How to Use This Flashcard System

  • Create Flashcards: Enter a term and its description, then click "Add Flashcard"
  • Study Mode: Click on the study card to flip it and reveal the answer
  • Navigation: Use the Previous and Next buttons to browse through your flashcards
  • Shuffle: Click the Shuffle button to randomize the order of your flashcards
  • Remove Cards: Click the "Remove" button on any flashcard to delete it
